About Us

We are a team of passionate developers who want to make the internet a safer place for everyone. We created Phishing Scanner+ to help users detect and avoid phishing scams that can compromise their personal information and security. Our app uses a machine learning model to analyze the links and compare them with a database of known phishing websites. We also provide useful tips and resources to help users protect themselves from phishing attacks.
